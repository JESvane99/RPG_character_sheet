Character sheet

Use this app to create and manage your characters for the game. Follow these steps:

  1. Use the form below to create a new character by entering a name and clicking "Create New".
  2. Click on a character's name in the navigation bar to view and edit their details.
  3. Navigate through the different sections (Description & Trappings, Skills & Talents, Action) to fill out all the necessary information for your character.
  4. Remember to save your changes by clicking the "Save Page" button on each page or hitting "Enter" from one of the number input fields.

Have fun!
