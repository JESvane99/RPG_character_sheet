Character sheet

Use this web application to create and manage your characters for the game. Follow these steps:

  1. Use the form on the left to create a new character by entering a name and clicking "New Character".
  2. Click on a character's name in the navigation bar to view and edit their details.
  3. Navigate through the different sections (Description & Trappings, Skills & Talents, Action) to fill out all the necessary information for your character.
  4. Remember to save your changes by clicking the "Save Page" button on each section.

Have fun!
